About Me

Ray

Hello, My name is Po Jui Cheng. You can also call me Ray. In Chinese, Jui and Ray are the same pronunciation. People often mispronounce it, and I can’t help but chuckle every time they do.

I’m a junior frontend developer with expertise in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I bring practical WordPress website development experience to the table. My passion for learning drives me to acquire new skills through online courses and community engagement. I prioritize writing clean, readable code and have honed my ability to optimize website performance from my previous experiences.
